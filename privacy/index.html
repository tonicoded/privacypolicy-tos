The 1To1 app does not collect, store, or share any personal data. We do not require account registration or access to personal identifiers.

We fetch live currency data from the public Frankfurter API. This is used solely for displaying exchange rates and is not stored or linked to users.

We do not use analytics tools, third-party tracking, or cookies. Your usage remains private and local to your device.

Exchange rate data may be temporarily cached locally on your device to improve performance, but this data is anonymous and not transmitted to us.
